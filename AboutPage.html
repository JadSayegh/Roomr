Goal
To create a platform for people to easily find apartments in neighbourhoods of their interest based on their budget and personal interests. After creating an account, a registered user can proceed to fill out a living preferences and an interests page which is then populated into the website's database. The user can at any time change his earlier requirements and perform another search. An algorithm is used to match the user's requirements against the information provided by other users which then returns search results for the request.

About Us
We are a team of McGill Engineering students who aim to make a website which can assist people with their apartment hunting process. We decided on a simple and clean look for the website to keep the focus on the website's purpose.
